# Will Larson

**Occupation:** Software Engineer, Author  
**Companies:** Yahoo!, Digg

## Bio

Will Larson is a software engineer and author known for his writings on system architecture and engineering management. He worked at Yahoo! and Digg before becoming an author, where he gained firsthand experience scaling systems under production pressure.

## Published Works

- **An Elegant Puzzle** — Systems engineering and management
- **Staff Engineer** — Leadership for senior engineers not on management track
- **The Engineering Executive's Primer** — Impactful leadership for engineering leaders
- **Crafting Engineering Strategy** — Engineering strategy development

## Key Contributions

Authored the influential article "Introduction to Architecting Systems for Scale" (2011), which documented foundational scalability patterns including:
- Load balancing strategies
- Caching layers (application, database, CDN)
- Offline processing with message queues
- Platform layer architecture
